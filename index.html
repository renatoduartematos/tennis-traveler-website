FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to bring my own racquet?+

No! All packages include quality loaner racquets in multiple grip sizes. If you prefer to bring your own, that's totally fine too.

What skill level is required?+

All skill levels are welcome — from complete beginners to advanced players. Just let us know when you book and we'll tailor the session accordingly.

Which hotels do you pick up from?+

We pick up from all major hotel corridors: International Drive, Lake Buena Vista, Disney Springs area, Universal area, and Downtown Orlando. If you're unsure, just ask!

What if it rains?+

Florida weather can be unpredictable! If your session is affected by rain, we'll reschedule at no charge or offer a full refund.

How do I pay?+

We use Stripe for secure online payments. You can pay with any major credit or debit card directly on the booking page.

Can I book for a large group or event?+

Absolutely! Our Corporate VIP package is designed for groups of 8+. Email us at info@thetennistraveler.com for a custom quote.
